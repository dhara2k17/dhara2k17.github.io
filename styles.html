Rules and Regulations

  1. Teams must consist of a minimum of 2 and maximum of 5 participants.
  2. Shortlisted teams from abstract submission round have to present their ideas in the form of power point presentation. The abstract should be submitted with minimum font size of 11 and single line spacing and must not exceed 3 pages. The abstract should be supported with valid references. Relevant statistics can be added to support your claim (you may add one extra page to include stats, images and hyperlink them wherever required).
  3. oThe event will be conducted in two phases:
    • Online submission of abstract
    • Final presentation by shortlisted teams
  4. Mail your submissions to raghavendra@jssaten.ac.in with subject as <Team name>_DHARA_2017 on or before 15 MARCH 2017, 11:59PM.
  5. All the teams are requested to provide contact numbers, Email IDs and name of the college of each member along with the attached submission file in the mail.
  6. The results of first round shall be given to the above listed e-mails and mobile numbers.

Tips

  1. Case study paper presentation involves the complete analysis of the topics where the given case study is analyzed from the various viewpoints it involves what was the condition of the area in past, what is its present scenario, what method and new technologies can be employed to improve it, if it is in degraded state and what measures are taken up by the government to improve the existing conditions.
  2. The topic you choose must be look into with greater detail it must encompass all the measures that have been taken and that are still in progress.
  3. If the report is supplemented by an additional reference paper it will provide an added advantage to the participants.
